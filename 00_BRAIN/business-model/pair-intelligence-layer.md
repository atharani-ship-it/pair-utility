# PAIR Intelligence Layer

## Purpose
PAIR is not only a metering and billing operator. Over time, PAIR should develop an intelligence layer that supports strategy, sales, operations, client communication, and tenant support.

The goal is to allow AI systems to assist the business while humans handle final decisions, approvals, physical work, and sensitive client relationships.

## Core Intelligence Areas

### 1. Strategic Thinking
The system should help PAIR evaluate:
- new commercial opportunities
- pricing structures
- partnership models
- risk allocation
- contract terms
- market positioning
- expansion opportunities

### 2. Economic & Financial Logic
The system should understand:
- chilled water billing models
- recovery of cooling costs
- meter service fees
- recurring revenue
- capex vs opex structures
- tenant billing impact
- mall/operator profitability
- collection risk

### 3. Operational Intelligence
The system should support:
- meter monitoring
- billing cycle checks
- anomaly detection
- maintenance alerts
- disconnection/reconnection workflows
- report generation
- issue escalation

### 4. Marketing & Sales Intelligence
The system should help PAIR:
- explain the service clearly
- create proposals
- create company profiles
- generate landing page content
- prepare sales emails
- respond to client objections
- position PAIR as a serious utility operator

### 5. Client-Facing Chatbot
Future website chatbot should support property owners, malls, developers, and facility managers.

It should be able to:
- explain BTU metering
- explain billing models
- collect project enquiry details
- qualify leads
- answer common questions
- route serious enquiries to PAIR team

It should not:
- give final pricing
- make legal commitments
- promise installation timelines
- approve commercial terms

### 6. Tenant-Facing Chatbot
Future tenant chatbot should support tenants after deployment.

It should be able to:
- explain invoices
- show usage summaries
- explain meter readings
- guide payment steps
- answer basic billing questions
- escalate disputes to human review

It should not:
- waive charges
- alter invoices
- promise reconnection
- make legal statements

## Human Approval Rules
AI can draft, analyse, and recommend.

Humans must approve:
- contracts
- tariffs
- disconnections
- legal notices
- client proposals
- final invoices before launch phase
- public statements

## Long-Term Goal
PAIR should become an AI-assisted utility operator where data, billing, operations, sales, and client support are connected through one controlled knowledge system.
